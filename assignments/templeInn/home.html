Washington DC Temple Facts

  • The DC Temple was the first temple built on the east coast.
  • Its six spire design was inspired by the Salt Lake City Temple.
  • The Washington DC Temple is the tallest temple.
  • It is the only Temple outside of Utah to have six ordinance rooms.
